Voice Notes

Voice Notes lets you record audio, transcribe it in real-time, and process the results with AI ‚Äî all on your device. Perfect for meetings, lectures, interviews, or quick voice memos.

Recording Audio

Navigate to the AI Voice Note tab and tap the record button to start capturing audio. The app uses WhisperKit for on-device speech recognition.

  • Real-time transcription: Text appears as you speak
  • Background recording: On iOS, recording continues when the app is in the background
  • Import audio: You can also import existing audio files for transcription
‚ÑπÔ∏è Privacy

All audio processing happens entirely on your device using WhisperKit. No audio data is sent to any server.

Transcription

Transcription is powered by the Whisper model running locally via WhisperKit. Features include:

  • Word-level timestamps: Each word is timestamped for precise navigation
  • Multiple languages: Support for many languages including English, Chinese, Japanese, Spanish, French, and more
  • Automatic punctuation: The model adds punctuation and sentence structure

AI Processing

After transcription, you can process the text with AI for:

  • Summarization: Get concise summaries of meetings or lectures
  • Translation: Translate the transcription to another language
  • Key points: Extract action items and key takeaways
  • Custom processing: Use any prompt to analyze the transcription

Word-Level Navigation

Tap any word in the transcription to jump to that exact moment in the recording. This makes it easy to:

  • Verify specific quotes or statements
  • Re-listen to important sections
  • Navigate long recordings efficiently

Language Support

WhisperKit supports transcription in many languages. The model automatically detects the spoken language, or you can set it manually for better accuracy with specific languages.
