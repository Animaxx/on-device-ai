Privacy Note

The Only AI App That Collects Zero Data

On Device AI Team May 30, 2026

Most "private" AI apps still phone home. Our App Store privacy label reads "Data Not Collected", not "Data Not Linked to You". Every model runs on-device and nothing ever leaves your hardware.

On Device AI enforces a true zero-data architecture: no remote accounts, no product analytics tracking, no third-party telemetry, and entirely sandboxed offline processing by default. Your ideas and conversations stay inside your own physical hardware.

When you look closely at the modern AI app landscape, "privacy" is frequently treated as a marketing gimmick. Countless tools boast about their private AI credentials but quietly execute remote scripts or route your sensitive document chats through third-party servers. When you check their privacy declarations, you encounter massive legal loopholes.

We engineered On Device AI on a completely different premise: that the only way to build a trustworthy local workspace is to collect absolutely nothing at all.

1. App Store Privacy Distinction: "Data Not Collected"

When you download apps from the Apple App Store, pay close attention to the App Privacy nutrition label. Most competitors use a standard cop-out rating: "Data Not Linked to You". This means they are still collecting your prompts, conversation histories, and device identifiers, but claiming they have separated the data from your official profile.

Our official App Store rating is: "Data Not Collected". We do not collect, store, or transmit a single byte of your data. There are simply no tracking pipelines in the app.

2. True Zero-Data & Zero-Telemetry Architecture

To deliver absolute privacy, we designed On Device AI with concrete technical boundaries:

  • No Product Analytics: We do not track which features you click, which models you download, or how many tokens you generate. Your usage patterns are your own business.
  • No Third-Party SDKs: We completely avoid embedding common analytics, tracking, or telemetry libraries from search engines or social media companies, ensuring zero background reporting.
  • No Server-Side Logs: Because your models run locally via GGUF or MLX on your own graphics cores, there is no remote server processing your requests or writing access logs.

3. No Account, No Registration, No Login

We don't want your email address, your phone number, or a password. You do not need to register or sign up to use On Device AI. Simply download the application, launch it, download your preferred open-source model, and begin working immediately.

Your entire conversation history, personalized knowledge databases, and custom settings remain encrypted and secured inside your system's sandboxed local database, managed via highly optimized local engines.

4. Bypassing the Cloud Completely

While cloud-based assistants continuously review your chats to refine their systems, your local models operate in a walled garden. Since all model execution happens entirely on-device, you can chat with high-confidentiality contracts, legal PDFs, and proprietary source code with absolute peace of mind.

On Device AI lets you reclaim your privacy while retaining the power of local language models. All processing stays exactly where it belongs: in your hands.
