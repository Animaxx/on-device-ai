Voice Workflows

Offline AI Meeting Transcription with Speaker Labels

Turn meetings, sensitive interviews, lectures, and quick audio memos into secure, local transcripts. Keep your confidential audio files entirely off the cloud.

Core Workflow: On Device AI Voice Notes execute Whisper-based speech-to-text models directly on your iPhone or Mac neural engine. By performing speaker diarization locally, it formats clean transcripts complete with speaker labels and summary metrics—all with 100% offline safety.

1. Secure Local Recording & Import

Start a secure session instantly by recording audio directly inside the app, or import pre-recorded files (MP3, M4A, WAV). Because all operations execute locally, there is zero risk of data leakage, making it perfect for private corporate meetings, client interviews, and confidential research.

2. Whisper-Powered Speech-to-Text

Our speech engine is powered by OpenAI's Whisper model optimized for Apple devices. By leveraging the Apple Neural Engine (ANE) on modern Apple Silicon chips, On Device AI provides highly accurate, real-time transcription locally, utilizing minimal battery and operating completely without network bandwidth.

3. Local Speaker Diarization

Tired of raw blocks of unlabelled text? The app includes localized Voice Activity Detection (VAD) and speaker clustering. The system segments the audio file, analyzes pitch and vocal characteristics, and automatically labels the transcript (e.g., Speaker 1, Speaker 2) to structure your transcripts naturally.

4. One-Click AI Summaries & Action Items

Once your transcript is generated, feed it directly into a local language model. Choose a specialized summary prompt or query your transcript directly in chat. Instantly generate meeting minutes, clear action items, or bulleted summaries offline with 100% data safety.
