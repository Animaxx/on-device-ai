Local AI for Apple Vision Pro

Bring private AI chat, secure local document libraries, and dynamic tool-calling interfaces into spatial computing. Pin your private workspaces anywhere in your room.

Core Spatial Workflow: Written natively for visionOS 2.0+, On Device AI introduces high-performance local AI to Apple Vision Pro. Seamlessly interact with local models, coordinate subagent Chat Flows, or offload heavy calculations to a local Mac server in your home network—ensuring complete spatial privacy.

1. Immerse Yourself in a Spatial AI Workspace

On Device AI on visionOS allows you to break free from single flat screens. Open multiple spatial windows: pin a local AI Chat session next to your browser, place an interactive vector document viewer next to your note-taking space, and coordinate multiple agent flows concurrently in the space around you.

2. Spatial Web Search & Tool Calling

Unleash the full capability of autonomous local agents in a spatial environment. Trigger advanced tool-calling actions (such as web search, directory reading, or mathematical calculations) that present formatted search results and citations suspended naturally in AR/VR windows.

3. Local Network Server Offloading

Executing massive language models inside a headset is constrained by physical thermal and battery limits. On Device AI solves this: you can configure an Apple Silicon Mac on your local network to act as an inference server. Your Vision Pro offloads the heavy LLM calculations securely over local Wi-Fi to your Mac, giving you lightning-fast inference, zero battery drag, and absolute offline privacy.

4. Secure spatial RAG with Knowledge Libraries

Import massive multi-page PDF collections, structural schematics, or saved web captures into a local Knowledge Library. Ground your spatial AI queries directly in your documents with 100% data safety, rendering responses clearly beside original source files.
